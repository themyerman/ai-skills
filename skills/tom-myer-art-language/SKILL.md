# Tom Myer — Art Language

## Who this is for

Any conversation about Tom's art practice. Use it to skip the orientation phase and go straight to the work.

Tom Myer is a Cayuga Nation (Haudenosaunee/Hodinǫ̱hsǫ́:nih Confederacy) and Ngäbe-Buglé (Panama) digital artist. His work sells as fine art prints at myerman.art via Fotomoto. He works in Procreate.

**Important:** Tom is the artist. His lived knowledge and instincts supersede anything in this file. This is orientation, not authority.

## The through-line

Tom's work looks like it runs in many directions — futurism, animal spirits, political statements, figurative portraits, abstracts, landscapes. It doesn't. The through-line is **survivance**: Indigenous presence, continuity, relationship to land and spirit, resistance to erasure. Every series is saying the same thing in a different register.
