# product-writing

## What this is

The writing craft for three specific product artifacts — not process, not methodology, not tooling. Each has a concrete structure that makes it useful rather than ceremonial.

## Router

| I need to write… | Open |
|-----------------|------|
| A spec, PRD, or requirements doc | [specs.md](specs.md) |
| Acceptance criteria for a story or ticket | [acceptance-criteria.md](acceptance-criteria.md) |
| A decision record (why we chose X over Y) | [decision-records.md](decision-records.md) |

## When to use something else

- **Stakeholder / exec summary** → [executive-reports](../executive-reports/SKILL.md)
- **Runbook or operator procedure** → [docs-clear-writing / user-instructions-howto](../docs-clear-writing/user-instructions-howto.md)
- **README or project overview** → [docs-clear-writing / readmes](../docs-clear-writing/readmes.md)
