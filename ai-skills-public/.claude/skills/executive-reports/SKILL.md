# executive-reports

## What this is

A pattern for **short, scannable** documents for **busy leads and program owners**: say what the artifact **is** in one breath, lead with the **so what**, separate **narrative** from **evidence dig**, and be explicit where numbers **do not** mean what people wish they meant.

**Layout patterns:** BLUF, “who should read which part,” partner or cross-team lens, **limitations** up front, long tables and raw evidence in **appendices**—see [reference.md](reference.md) for section templates and anti-patterns.

## When to use

- A **new** executive summary, **rollup** (logs, model runs, campaign results), or **“read this first”** slice of a long analysis
- **Rewriting** a dense technical doc into a leadership-facing version **without** losing the caveats
- **Defining** what goes in the main body **vs** appendices for **PDF / email** vs **drill-down**
